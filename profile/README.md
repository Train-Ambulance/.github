## Practical Tips for Families Considering Train Ambulance from Patna

If you or your family ever face a medical emergency requiring long-distance transfer, here are a few practical tips to make the process smoother:

1. **Call Early:** Contact the train ambulance provider as soon as the doctor recommends transfer. Early coordination ensures timely train reservation and setup.  
2. **Keep Documents Ready:** Always have the patient’s ID proof, recent medical reports, and doctor’s referral letter ready.  
3. **Share Full Details:** Inform the ambulance team about the patient’s condition — oxygen level, ventilator requirement, or medication.  
4. **Accompany with Caregiver:** One or two family members can travel along, but they should follow medical staff instructions strictly.  
5. **Confirm Bed at Destination Hospital:** Before departure, book an ICU bed at the receiving hospital to avoid last-minute delays.  

These small steps can **save precious time — and lives.**
